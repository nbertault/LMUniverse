Aston Martin Vantage GT3

With such an illustrious history competing in the GT class at Le Mans, it’s no surprise the renowned brand is represented in the LMGT3 class.
The latest second-generation Aston Martin Vantage AMR GT3 succeeds the successful Vantage GTE in the World Endurance Championship which scored a remarkable 52 class wins and 11 world titles between 2012 and 2023. With familiar teams Heart of Racing and D’Station on the grid, the new car is already off to winning ways having chalked up one win at COTA in 2024.
With a stable and predictable platform and the V8 thunder, the Vantage will continue it’s popularity in game and with Le Mans Ultimate players.
