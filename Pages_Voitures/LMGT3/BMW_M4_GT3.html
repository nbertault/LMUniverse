BMW M4 GT3

First homologated in 2022, the BMW M4 GT3 is the latest from a lineage of BMW GT cars including the Z4 GT3, M6 GT3 and M8 GTE. Returning to Le Mans and the World Endurance Championship in GT after a 5 year absence, the brand partnered with sportscar ace team WRT, and gave a set of keys to 9 x Motorcycle World Champion Valentino Rossi.
The BMW M4 GT3 had already proven its pace in competition in other GT series before FIA WEC, but with a 1 – 2 finish in Imola and a strong showing in Le Mans the team came away with 4th overall in the classification.
