Peugeot 9X8 2024

The evolution era has begun in the FIA World Endurance Championship, with Peugeot parking its ground effect, wingless wonder for a new-look purposeful 9X8 for 2024 with 90% of the bodywork completely redefined.
In 2024 Peugeot Sport started its campaign with the 2023 car and challenged for the win in Qatar. The #93 car was on course for second place – a dream result to start the season – but power failure saw them not finish the race.
When the series retuned to Europe, the French team pulled the covers off its 2024 9X8 – with the much talked about rear wing making its first appearance. The #93 car managed a solid top result on its debut with two more top ten results following for the #93 with ninth at Spa and eighth in Brazil – the best result for the car to date. at the Italian circuit.
Luck hasn’t blessed the #94 car with the team struggling to match the pace of the sister car with a best result of 11th at Le Mans the highlight so far. The team are looking horns with some of the worlds biggest automotive car makers and sit sixth in the constructors championship.
Peugeot are performing live testing at every venue this year, gathering data and knowledge – and sees 2024 as a development year for the car with eyes fully on making an impact in 2025.
