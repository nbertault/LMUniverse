Aston Martin Valkyrie AMR-LMH

The Aston Martin Valkyrie AMR- LMH marks the British marque’s return to top-class endurance racing in the FIA World Endurance Championship (WEC). Based on the radical road-going Valkyrie hypercar, the LMH version is developed in collaboration with Heart of Racing and engineered by Aston Martin Performance Technologies.
The project revives Aston Martin’s ambitions after its previous Valkyrie-based Hypercar program was paused in 2020. Re-entering competition in 2025, the Valkyrie AMR-LMH features a naturally aspirated V12 engine, adapted for endurance racing, and competes against other factory-backed entries in the Hypercar class.
While both cars have successfully completed every race since Qatar, the pace and competitiveness is still to be found with P12 in the 2025 24 Hours Le Mans the highest finishing position so far. The Valkyrie LMH aims to reestablish Aston Martin as a top contender on the world stage of endurance racing.
